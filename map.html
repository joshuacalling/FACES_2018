THE THEME


Hakuna Matata! What a wonderful phrase! Hakuna Matata! Ain’t no passing craze. It means no worries, for the rest of your days. It’s our problem free; philosophy Hakuna Matata! By Timon and Pumba FACES has annually been a host to a mixture of events. As we enter our 25th iteration we wish to incorporate a silver lining to our cloud and make the few days of our busy life trouble free. This FACES we present to you HAKUNA MATATA days of our life. HAKUNA MATATA tells us to live freely, and enjoy every minute of life rather than worrying about things.
HAKUNA MATATA is a phrase that all of us students have been listening to throughout our childhood, evoking a strong feeling of nostalgia. This time we have included as much input as we could get from the entire college, in virtue of molding every event to cater to every need.
We have loads of sports and cultural events aimed to bring students from every branch together. We aim to develop each of our students holistically so that the challenges they may face in events prepare them to handle their future.
Every event has been crafted meticulously, so that everyone can participate. Also, there will be “no worries” as we have included a plethora of opportunities throughout FACES. This year FACES promises to be an ode to the stress-free days of childhood
